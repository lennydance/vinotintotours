VINO TINTO TOURS

PROVIDING CUSTOM WINE TOURS THROUGH THE GUADALUPE VALLEY WINE COUNTRY NORTHERN BAJA CALIFORNIA

The quality and variety of the vineyards in the Guadalupe Valley of Northern Baja California are grossly underestimated and regrettably undiscovered by many who come here to tour the Wine Country. In an effort to maximize profit, many Tour Companies are driving by some of the best wines and vineyards in Mexico. Vino Tinto Tours was started in the hopes of changing this trend. We provide knowledge and insight on how to truly discover what the Guadalupe Valley has to offer. Our tours allow you to control the level of discovery that appeals to your tastes. Contact us to enjoy a casual wine tour off the beaten path.
